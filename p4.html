Space Boy!

Space Boy is a 2D pixel platformer game that draws inspiration from classic games such as Super Mario. The game comprises of three playable levels, including a formidable boss fight, and is readily accessible on itch.io. Through the development of this project, I gained hands-on experience in scripting enemy behavior, implementing intricate movement mechanics such as double jumps, designing levels, creating pick-up systems, incorporating environmental damage, and developing elevators, keys, and doors. Additionally, I acquired a broad range of new skills and knowledge in game development.
