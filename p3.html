Space Astra

Space Astra is a 2D pixel space shooter developed with PhotoShop and Unity, drawing inspiration from classic arcade and console games. The game features three levels, including a challenging boss fight, and is readily available for play on itch.io. Through this project, I acquired a comprehensive understanding of sound design, game borders, spawners, health and life systems, timers, and various other key aspects of game development.
