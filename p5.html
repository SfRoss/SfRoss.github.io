Bug Planet

Bug Planet is a 3D Low Poly First-Person Shooter (FPS) game, inspired by classic FPS games such as Doom and Quake. The game features two levels and a boss fight. This project allowed me to enhance my knowledge in working with Low Poly graphics, and gain insights into the creation of 3D enemy behavior, multi-level design, and visual effects. Additionally, I was able to gain practical experience in implementing first-person controls and camera settings.
