Pirate Themed Pixel Island

This project was a demonstration of 3D low poly design, executed with Maya and imported into Unity. The game was crafted as a third-person pirate-themed experience, showcasing low poly assets and a character with animations for idle, walking, and jumping actions. The challenge of creating every object, including the island and the character, from scratch, as well as the process of weight painting, rigging, and animation, added to the overall complexity of the project.
